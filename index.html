Focused upon sustainability, DFI offers a patented shopping innovation that grows revenue, supported by a full range of certified products specifically designed for the Travel Retail industry.

Our environmentally friendly innovations are used by leading Travel Retailers and brands. Providing a hands free shopping experience, our products are driven by shopper insight and are proven to grow sales and increase spend.

Our Mission.

Embracing the challenge to reduce single use plastic in Travel Retail, DFI is committed to supporting the industry objective to save 1 billion plastic bottles from landfill by 2025.

Our range of certified 91% rPET recycled products directly prevent plastic bottles from reaching landfill.

We are encouraging airports, retailers and brands to align with our missions to remove, to re-use and to recycle plastic from the supply chain.

Quality and Assurance.

Our unique Travel Bags are fully certified to the highest standards.

Our supply chain, manufacturing facilities and processes are closely monitored and audited to ensure quality and guarantee accreditation from international environmental agencies.
