DFI has created and patented a unique concept for Travel Retail.

Our Travel Basket and Travel Bag system is specifically designed to make shopping easy for travellers.

Our system is based on leading shopper insight and is proven to drive sales, encourage trade up and satisfy shopper demand for a greener alternative way to shop.

The Travel Basket

Beautifully created and handmade specifically for Travel Retail, the Travel Basket fits most trolley bag designs. Proven to drive sales, the Travel Basket makes shopping easy and efficient.

Versatile and unique, the Travel Basket offers multiple opportunities for personalisation, branding, advertising and sponsorship.

Shopping

  • Enables hands free shopping.
  • Encourages more browsing and makes the purchasing of multiple items easy and simple.

Brand exposure

  • Ability to fully customise the colour, logo and brand message.
  • Communication can be added to the front and back panel zones.

Commercial revenue

  • Includes space for brand advertising and messages.
  • Creates a revenue opportunity when the space is offered to brand sponsors.

The Travel Bag

A patented design, created specifically with ease-of-shop for the traveller in mind, the Travel Bag also meets all cabin-approved guidelines. Perfect for carrying multiple items, the Travel Bag can easily accommodate products of all sizes.

Certified 91% rPET and fully recyclable, the Travel Bag is not only the most ecological bag available in Travel Retail but also provides a unique opportunity for mass exposure.

With the opportunity to place the bag in millions of shopper’s hands, the Travel Bag provides immediate brand impact and long lasting brand advertising.

Retail Branded Bag

Can be fully customised to drive promotional awareness and stand-out of any brand or message.

Can be easily changed to meet the needs of different shopper groups, promotional activity or messaging.

Gift with purchase

Low cost versus traditional GWP options, representing excellent value.

Increase awareness and brand identity through bespoke bag design and print, generating stand out while proven to drive product engagement.

Shopper Insight:

48% of all shoppers only purchase one item.
Only 6% of shoppers buy from more than one category.
52% of all shoppers say they “Are not able to carry any more items”.
The inability to carry more items is the number 1 reason that Chinese shoppers do not buy more.

Source: DFI Travel Industry Research
