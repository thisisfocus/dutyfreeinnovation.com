Customers

We work with global leaders in travel including airports, tour operators and other international businesses looking to provide their customers with value added services.

Schiphol Amsterdam Airport

Schiphol first purchased the travel bag in 2015, and implemented the solution throughout the airport in 2016. Delighted with the success, Schiphol are to make the Travel Bag a permanent part of their passenger service offer. Schiphol is the third largest airport in Europe, catering primarily for Amsterdam, capital of the Netherlands.

Visit the Schiphol web site

Thomas Cook

Thomas Cook Airlines Scandinavia is a Danish charter airline headquartered in Copenhagen and part of the Thomas Cook Group. It operates flights to leisure destinations mainly in the Mediterranean as well as some long-haul service from several bases in the Nordic Countries. The travel bag is used within the pre-order Duty Free sales market, for delivery to the passengers when they have boarded the aircraft.
