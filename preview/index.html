At Duty Free Innovation we are designing products and solutions to overcome the challenges of driving sales in global travel retail.

The Travel Basket

Less than 10% of passengers today use a shopping basket, this results in a massive lost opportunity as those who do use a basket will spend +301% more than average.

The Travel Basket enables hand-free browsing of the store, and is custom designed for the travel retail market.

The Travel Bag

Already successfully implemented at Heathrow and Schiphol Airports.

Provides passengers with an easy-to-use solution for transporting goods purchased in duty-free outlets.

An average transaction value increase of 13% where the bag was provided and purchased.
