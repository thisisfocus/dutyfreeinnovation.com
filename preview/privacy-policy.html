Our Privacy Policy

Duty Free Innovation appreciate that your privacy is important to you. Any information that you choose to submit through this web site - such as your name and email address - will be treated with the strictest confidence.

In addition:

  • we do not sell our names and addresses to any external third parties.
  • emails sent by us to you as part of any news updates, promotions or marketing campaigns will include an opportunity and instructions on how to 'unsubscribe' from future mailings.
  • you may also cancel your subscription to any mailings at any time by sending your instructions to us at info@dutyfreeinnovation.com

However we do reserve the right to disclose user information when we believe in good faith that the law requires it.

Our web site collects some information in an automated manner from all visitors - such as visitor numbers, IP address and browsers used. In no way will this information identify you personally. The data is stored in log files and is used by us for analysis in order for us to deliver an enhanced experience for web site users.

Parts of our web site use cookies but these do not identify you personally and are only used to identify trends in how our site is used. You may disable cookies at any time in your browser settings.

By using this web site you signify your acceptance of this Privacy Policy. We reserve the right to modify the contents of this Policy from time to time.
