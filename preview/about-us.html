Meet the Team

Chris Powell
CEO & Founder

Chris has undertaken strategic leadership tasks in global travel retail since 2001, most notably at BATI and JTI. Chris relishes embracing the challenges and opportunities that the industry offers, which he believes to be profound. This motivation drives the DFI concept, an assembled management team each contributing unique perspectives to find solutions and deliver value.

Chris is married with three young children, who live in very rural France, and he maintains a keen interest in history. Chris can be contacted via email or on +41 78 704 8530

Darren Eaton
Chief Operating Officer

Darren has spent over 20 years working in travel retail, mainly for British American Tobacco, most recently as the Head of Trade for Global Travel Retail. Darren has extensive knowledge of the travel retail market, and has also lived and worked in the United Kingdom, Spain, Dubai and Switzerland.

Darren is a keen runner and Arsenal fan, he is married with two children and lives in Zurich, Switzerland. Darren can be contacted via email or via +41 79 779 44 66.

Martin Braddock
Chairman

Martin is an entrepreneur who is both invested in and involved in the direction of many companies, across business segments. As both a Chartered Accountant and the holder of a Bachelor of Laws Degree, Martin has worked for Price Waterhouse and Japan Tobacco International throughout an extensive career which has taken him from the UK, to Hungary and eventually to Switzerland. Martin is passionate about businesses, top line and people growth has 11 years of Board experience working within major, multinational organisations. Also, a keen runner, Martin is also a rugby fan and enjoys skiing during the winter. Martin resides in Switzerland and is also married, with two children.

Ross Doyle
Chief Financial Officer

Ross has worked in many organisations during his career, focusing in the field of Financial Management. In his latest role, he was CFO within the Coal Division of Glencore, a global commodities producer and trader, operating in over 50 countries.

Ross is based in Switzerland, and is married with three children. Additionally, Ross is a multi-event endurance athlete, who has completed the Ironman Triathlon event on numerous occasions.
